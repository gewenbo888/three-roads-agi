# Three Roads to AGI — 通往AGI的三条路

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-three--roads--agi.vercel.app-d4a840)](https://three-roads-agi.vercel.app)

A bilingual (Chinese–English) deep dive into the three competing approaches to Artificial General Intelligence and what all three are missing.

## The Three Roads

| Road | Champion | Core Belief |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Reinforcement Learning** | Demis Hassabis (DeepMind) | Intelligence = learning from interaction with environments |
| **World Models** | Yann LeCun (Meta AI) | Intelligence = building internal models that predict the world |
| **Language Models** | Sam Altman / Dario Amodei | Intelligence = compression and generation of language |

## Sections

- **Road I: Reinforcement Learning** — AlphaGo to AlphaFold, strengths, weaknesses, milestones
- **Road II: World Models** — JEPA architecture, biological plausibility, the gap between vision and execution
- **Road III: Language Models** — The scaling hypothesis, real-world traction, the hallucination problem
- **The Missing Piece** — Survival instinct, embodiment, temporal experience, subjective experience
- **Comparison Table** — 10-dimension side-by-side comparison
- **Synthesis** — Why AGI may need all three plus something we haven't identified

## Design

Dark void aesthetic with three-color system (red/RL, blue/World Models, green/Language), converging particle paths hero canvas, Instrument Serif + Atkinson Hyperlegible + Noto Serif SC typography.
